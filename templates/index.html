About Us

Welcome to MindSpectra

Welcome to MindSpectra, a website created by a team of students from VIT Chennai, India. Our goal is to bridge the gap between doctors and society by spreading awareness and knowledge about Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD). As individuals who have witnessed the impact of ASD on our friends and families, we understand the importance of early detection and intervention. Therefore, we have created tools to detect mu rhythm and classify autism.

Our team is dedicated in providing accessible and reliable information about ASD, including its symptoms, causes, and treatments. We believe that by empowering individuals with knowledge, we can create a more inclusive and understanding society for those with ASD..

At MindSpectra, we value collaboration and community. We welcome feedback and suggestions from doctors, caregivers, and individuals with ASD to improve our resources and services. Our team is committed to continuously learning and updating our platform to ensure that it remains a valuable resource for all. Thank you for visiting MindSpectra. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of those with ASD.
